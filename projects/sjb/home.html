About Us
"I have found that many profess to be carpenters, when they are not. Stu is a carpenter and works to a very high standard."
- Mr S. Young, Cheltenham

SJB Carpentry & Joinery specialises in bespoke furniture making. We feel that by working closely with you throughout the process of design and creation, the result is a unique, one-of-a-kind piece of craftsmanship.

Based in Churchdown, Gloucester, we work to specifically suit individual needs with our custom craftwork, covering all aspects of home improvement and maintenance - from entire bespoke kitchens to beautiful handcrafted doors, and so much more.

Our aim is to meet all customer requests, providing a finished product of unrivalled quality. We pride ourselves on offering an affordable, professional and friendly service.

A selection of our previous projects are available to view below - please feel free to use these as inspiration. Any item can be recreated, or aspects can be taken and used in new projects. Don't hesitate to get in touch, as all quotes are free of charge with no obligation to proceed with works.
